## Introduction to MARBLE

 Music Audio Representation Benchmark for universaL Evaluation (MARBLE) is a benchmark proposed to help the academic & industrial to study, compare, and select pre-trained models according comprehensive performances on a wide range of Music Information Retrieval (MIR) tasks.
